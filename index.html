About Me

Currently, I am human resources assistant at a caregiving company. I have a bachelor's degree in Anthropology with Human Biology emphasis. I have worked in the heathcare field for 6 years under different titles. I have worked in training, recruiting and hiring caregivers. I am looking to learn coding to be able to use my experience and knowledge of the field to build and improve tools that these professionals use everyday. I am a mother to a 22 month old and a wife to a 26 year old.I have two dogs a german shephard and a chiweenie dog. I am originally from Arizona but have been living in the Sacramento area for 3 years. I really do enjoy living in this area and hope to use all of my knowledge to help create something great.
